Subhas Chandra Bose

1897 - 1945

Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!

Biography

Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist whose defiant patriotism made him a hero in India. He was a prominent figure in the Indian independence movement against British rule. Bose was the leader of the Indian National Army (INA), which was allied with the Axis powers during World War II. He is best known for his advocacy of armed struggle for Indian independence and his famous slogan, "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!" Bose's legacy continues to inspire many in India and around the world.
